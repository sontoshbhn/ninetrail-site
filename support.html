Frequently asked questions

Does NineTrail work offline?

Yes. The entire game — daily puzzle, unlimited practice, the Coach, and your statistics — works fully offline. No account or sign-in is required.

How does the Coach work?

When you tap Coach, NineTrail explains the next logical step in stages: first the idea, then the cell to focus on, and finally the number with a short explanation. Every hint is worked out on your device.

How do I reset my statistics or all data?

Open Settings → Data. You can reset statistics, or reset all app data (saved game, statistics, streaks, and preferences). Deleting the app also removes everything stored on your device.

Are there ads?

No. This version of NineTrail contains no advertising.

Is there a Pro version?

NineTrail is free and fully playable. An optional “NineTrail Pro” upgrade may be offered in a future update to add extras such as advanced coaching, expert packs, the full daily archive, deeper statistics, and more themes. The core game always stays free.

I changed devices — is my progress saved?

Progress is stored locally on each device, so it does not currently transfer between devices. Cloud sync may be added in a future update.

What about my privacy?

NineTrail collects no personal data and requests no sensitive permissions. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Accessibility

NineTrail includes scalable text, a high-contrast mode, colour-blind-safe indicators, screen-reader labels, and large touch targets. If something is hard to use, please email us — accessibility feedback is very welcome.
