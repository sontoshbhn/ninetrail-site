Privacy Policy

App: NineTrail — Daily Number Logic Coach  ·  Developer: Sontosh Bhandari
Effective date: June 3, 2026  ·  Contact: sontoshbhn@gmail.com

The short version. NineTrail does not collect your personal data. The game is fully playable offline, requires no account, and keeps your puzzles, progress, settings, and statistics on your device. It contains no advertising and no analytics. We never request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, photos, location, calendar, SMS, or Bluetooth.

1. Data we collect

None. NineTrail operates no servers for the game and makes no network requests to us. It does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our systems.

2. Data stored on your device only

The app saves the following locally, using your operating system's standard app storage. It never leaves your device through the app:

  • your in-progress puzzle and game saves;
  • gameplay statistics (games played, win rate, best times, streaks);
  • preferences (theme, accessibility options, gameplay toggles).

You can erase all of it at any time in Settings → Data → Reset all app data, or by uninstalling the app.

3. Advertising

This version of NineTrail contains no advertising. No advertising software is included in the app, no ad is shown, and no advertising identifier is used. If a future version introduces optional ads, this policy and the app's store privacy disclosures will be updated before that version is released, and you will be given in-app controls.

4. Analytics and crash reporting

None. This version includes no analytics or crash-reporting software. No usage or diagnostic data is sent anywhere.

5. Purchases

If NineTrail offers an optional “Pro” upgrade and you choose to buy it, the purchase is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We never see or store your name, address, or payment details. The app receives only an anonymous confirmation from the store that a purchase or restore succeeded, used to unlock features on your device. Apple's and Google's own privacy policies govern their processing.

6. Children

NineTrail is intended for a general audience aged 13 and older. It is not directed to children and, because it collects no personal data, does not knowingly process data from children.

7. Your rights and choices

Because your game data lives only on your device, you can exercise the essential rights directly: your data is on your device (access/portability), you can delete it via Settings → Data → Reset or by uninstalling, and there is nothing collected to opt out of. If your region's law (for example GDPR, UK GDPR, or California's CCPA/CPRA) grants additional rights, contact us and we will respond within the required time.

8. Security

We minimise risk by minimising data: nothing is collected, so nothing can be exposed from our side. On-device data is stored in your operating system's standard protected app storage and is removed when you delete the app.

9. Future features (advance notice)

If a future update introduces optional advertising, optional analytics or crash reporting, or any cloud backup or sync, we will revise this policy and the store disclosures before that update is released.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. The effective date above will change, and material changes will be noted in the app's release notes or store listing.
